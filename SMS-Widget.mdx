## ðŸ“² Deploy Your SMS Widget

 1. Click on **Widgets** from the Left Panel

In the **Assistants** section of the left panel, click on **Widgets**. This will take you to the widget setup page where you can deploy your AI assistant.

> ðŸ”” **Note:** Ensure you have already created an AI chat assistant. If not, please follow the previous guides to create and configure your assistant first.

---

 2. Create New Widget

Click **Create New Widget** to start the setup process.

![Create New Widget](images/sms/2.png)

---

 3. Select **Twilio** as the Provider Type

In the widget setup screen:

- Choose **Twilio** as the provider type  
- Click **Next** to proceed

![Select Provider](images/sms/3.png)

---

 4. Select Widget Type, Name, and Description

- **Widget Type:** Choose `SMS`  
- **Name:** Enter a descriptive name (e.g., *Customer Support Chat*)  
- **Description:** Add a brief description (e.g., *Handles customer support inquiries via SMS*)

Click **Next** to move on.

![Widget Type and Info](images/sms/4.png)

---

 5. Select Your Chat Assistant

From the dropdown list, choose the AI assistant you previously created.

![Select Assistant](images/sms/5.png)

---

 6. Configure Twilio SMS Widget

- Select your **Twilio Account** from the dropdown  
- Choose a **Phone Number** associated with that account  
- Click **Finish** to complete the deployment

![Configure Widget](images/sms/6.png)

---
